Introduction

Since temperatures go up, we are already in Spring, and we will be soon in summer, the fashion companies have started adapting their collections.

For that reason, Massimo Dutti, as every almost every year, has launcher their limited edition for the Spring Summer, with a clear nod to summer and fresh style.

Massimo Dutti presented some days ago (end of March) the Limited Edition SS22 and it is already available for purchase. For this time, taking advantage of the rising temperatures, the collection is set in the Maghreb region.

Since this post is mainly about the different new looks and articles, it will be shorter than other post of this website

Coming back to the collection, the campaign photography is made by Josh Olins and the thematic for this campaign is linen, nature, and summery vibes. That’s why most of the shots are taken in desert landscapes of the North African region.

In this collection, Massimo Dutti focuses on the main characteristics, with no specific preference:

  • Purity and nature
  • Sharp silhouettes
  • Natural colors
  • Nods to Craftsmanship
  • Light garments
  • Comfortable fabrics
  • Classic patterns
  • Avant garde point of view

These attributes are aligned with the brand’s principles and culture. At the same time, Massimo Dutti still bets on the look of a sober man with classic taste, as well as cosmopolitan and open to change. You can see also this culture applied in other campaigns such as the Join Life collections

The preferred materials are also focused on nature and adapt to warmer months of the year. That’s why MD has opted for materials such as linen, leather, or different blends of knitwear.

Aligned with choice of the materials and landscapes, they try to evoke non artificial man. As opposed to other brands, it is difficult that you will see synthetic materials in the fabrics, and you will mainly see natural fabrics such as the ones mentioned for this collection.

My favorite pieces of the collection

In this post, I am selecting the most charismatic and unique pieces from this collection. However, I invite you to visit the website to find any other garment that could be interesting for you too.

Single breasted is still the preferred choice for most men. However, this double-breasted blazer can be a good item to distinguish from other typical suits. You can combine with the trousers of the same blazer or with more casual garments such as jeans.

Leather Espadrilles

The known as Alpargatas in Spain, it is a typical product from Spain, used in this country, the south of France and some regions of America. Known as a humble shoe and evolved from the Egyptian and Roman sandals, Massimo Dutti has redesigned this shoe for this collection. This model is kept simple, but adds some sophisticated final touches, resembling in some cases the woven design of Bottega Venetta.
