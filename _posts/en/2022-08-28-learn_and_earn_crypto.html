Learn to Earn Programs

The first group of this article is related to those programs where you learn the theory, or the basic concepts of a given blockchain or token. In the subsections below, you don’t just learn something new, but you also have the chance of getting rewarded with crypto currencies.

In the Learning Rewards Section you can see different articles, courses and quizzes

These are some of the most famous Learn to Earn programs out there, where you learn interesting new topics and you are rewarded in different manners.

Please bear in mind that these courses do not have any requirements such as “deposit 10 to earn 20, etc.”. Apart from learning and reading/watching the information, the only other requirement is that in some cases you may need to verify and pass the KYC checks.


Coinbase

Coinbase is sometimes criticized for different reasons such as business-related topics (dismissal of employees, business plan) or the trading fees. However, since they started in 2018, they have promoted different initiatives in the crypto community. One of the most famous ones is the Learning Rewards program, where users can earn different tokens. The process is quite easy and just watching a short video and answering some questions you can get some free tokens.

There is no surprise here and you can exchange as soon as you receive. The rewards are not a big amount, but, lately, they tend to occur quite often. Regardless of the rewards, videos are nice to watch and very well done. In addition to these videos, you may also see different articles.

Cake DeFi

Cake DeFi is a quite new player in the Decentralized Finance sector and now quite known for many people. However, with their current solutions, it is likely that you will get hear from them more and more. They are a company based in Singapore specialized in passive income, being the main pillars of these platform the following s

1. Liquidity mining: as opposed to centralized exchanges, in a DEX, liquidity is needed in order to enable trades between the different pairs. With liquidity mining you provide your crypto assets so other can use these to trade.

2. Staking: Via the Proof of Stake protocol, you lock your tokens to protect the network., and you get rewarded with additional tokens

3. Lending: The last strategy one is income generated from lending your coins to other DeFi participants.

With the available courses, you can at the moment earn the following tokens.

  • DFI (DeFiChain) tokens
  • BTC-DFI Tokens

Both of them are allocated to Staking and Liquidity mining, but after you unfreeze them you can do whatever you want with the coins you got in the courses plus the ones you got from the freeze period.

There is also a course coming soon related to Ethereum, where you will also learn something new and get rewarded.

Revolut

Revolut is very good for many aspects such as banking, currency conversion, travelling and the super cool features of the app such as virtual cards or instant transfers. However, when it comes to their investment features such as purchasing stock or crypto, there are still some aspects that should be improved.

That’s probably why in order to take off, Revolut has launched a Learn & Earn Program related to Polkadot. Compared to other courses, the lessons are related to one of the most important blockchains and the amounts are also higher.

In addition to the videos, you have also the option to see the glossary, where some important crypto and blockchain terms are explained.

There is no referral program here and you will get those tokens for you. You decide if you want to sell them for fiat or exchange them the moment you receive them.

Ethernaut

Probably you have been hearing recently about the Ethereum Merge and the consequences that the fork may have not just for the Ethereum network but the whole crypto scene. Ethernaut is a war game played in an Ethereum Virtual Machine. developed by the OpenZeppelin, Ethernaut is a Web3 application developed with Solidity, where you can also learn this programming language.

The purpose of the game is both having fun and learning about Ethereum with different coding exercises at the same time you assimilate understand the Ethereum concepts with some practice.

Crypto Zombies

Similar to Ethernaut, Crypto Zombies is a game where you can build a Zombie Factory to build your personal army composed of unique zombies.

Once you have created the zombies, the fun is not over. It’s now where you can fight other learners’ armies. Furthermore, you also get unique collectibles like other games. As with Ethernaut, you will also learn blockchain specific topics such as ERC721, Web3 or zkSync, making this website quite complete in order to have basic knowledge about dApp creation.
